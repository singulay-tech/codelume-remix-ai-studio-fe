# Codelume Privacy Policy

**Version: 2026.05.18**  
**Effective date: May 18, 2026**  
**Operator: Beijing Xingjieli Technology Co., Ltd.** (“we”, “us”, or “our”)

We value your privacy. This policy explains how Codelume and related online services collect, use, store, and protect your information.

## 1. Information we collect

### 1.1 Information you provide

- **Account information**: You must use Sign in with Apple to use Codelume. We may receive identifiers, email (if you authorize), and display names from Apple to create and maintain your Codelume account;
- **Profile**: Optional nickname, avatar URL, profile background, and similar fields;
- **Submissions and interactions**: Metadata for uploaded wallpapers, policy consent records, likes, follows, download history, and related data;
- **Contact and feedback**: Content you send by email or in support communications.

### 1.2 Information collected automatically

- **Device and logs**: App version, OS version, request timestamps, error logs, and similar data to keep the service secure and stable;
- **Network information**: IP addresses and related data needed for downloads, uploads, and authentication (processed by infrastructure providers);
- **Usage statistics**: Aggregated feature usage (not used to identify you individually as a primary purpose).

### 1.3 Information we do not intentionally collect

Codelume does not require government ID numbers, bank card numbers, or precise geolocation. Please do not disclose unnecessary personal information in public submission descriptions.

## 2. How we use information

We use the information above to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve wallpaper browsing, downloading, submission, review, and account features;
- Authenticate users and manage security risks (e.g. preventing bulk scraping or upload abuse);
- Comply with legal obligations and handle complaints and infringement notices;
- Send important service-related notices where you have consented.

## 3. Sharing and processors

We do not sell your personal information. We may share or delegate processing only when:

- **Infrastructure providers** such as Supabase (database and auth) and Cloudflare (storage and CDN) need data to operate the service;
- **Apple**: Codelume accounts can only be created and authenticated through Sign in with Apple; Apple processes information you provide to Apple under Apple’s privacy policy;
- **Legal requirements** apply when courts or authorities lawfully request information.

We use contracts and configuration to require reasonable security measures from processors.

## 4. Storage and cross-border transfer

Data is primarily stored in the People’s Republic of China or regions where our chosen cloud services operate. We retain data for as long as needed for this policy’s purposes or as required by law. After account deletion or as required by law, we delete or anonymize data.

## 5. Your rights

Where applicable law allows, you may:

- Access and correct your account information (in the app or by email);
- Withdraw certain consents (some features may become unavailable);
- Request deletion of your account and related personal data (subject to legal exceptions);
- Ask questions or file complaints about this policy.

Email **codelume@163.com** and we will respond within a reasonable period.

## 6. Minors

Codelume is intended primarily for adults. Minors should read this policy and use the service with a guardian. If we learn we collected a minor’s information without guardian consent, we will delete it promptly.

## 7. Cookies and similar technologies

Our website (codelume.cn) may use browser local storage or similar technologies to remember language preferences and maintain sessions. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

## 8. Policy updates

We may update this policy and publish a new version and effective date on this page. For material changes, we will notify you through the app or website. Continued use means you understand the updated policy.
