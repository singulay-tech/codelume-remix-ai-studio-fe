# Codelume UGC Guidelines

**Version: 2026.05.18**  
**Effective date: May 18, 2026**  
**Operator: Beijing Xingjieli Technology Co., Ltd.** (“we”, “us”, or “our”)

These guidelines apply when you submit dynamic wallpapers (user-generated content, “UGC”) to Wallpaper Hub through the Codelume app. Before submitting, you must agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

## 1. Eligibility

- You have registered a Codelume account through Sign in with Apple and have accepted the current policy versions;
- Your account is not restricted or banned;
- You have lawful rights or authorization for the content you submit.

## 2. Content and format requirements

- Submissions must use the **.bundle** package or other formats we officially support, with required previews and asset packages;
- Metadata (name, description, category, dimensions, etc.) must be accurate and not misleading;
- Content must not include malware or material that infringes others’ intellectual property or privacy;
- Do not submit content prohibited by law or content that is pornographic, excessively violent, hateful, discriminatory, or otherwise inappropriate.

## 3. Copyright and license

At submission you must select a copyright declaration type (e.g. original work, licensed, public domain) and provide accurate information. You represent that:

- You own the copyright or have necessary authorization for the submission;
- You grant us a non-exclusive license needed to display, distribute, and promote the work on Wallpaper Hub (including storage, transcoding, preview generation, and downloads for users);
- If third-party assets are included, you have obtained authorization and can disclose it as required by these guidelines.

## 4. Review and publication

After submission, your work enters **pending review**. We may approve or reject based on quality, compliance, and technical requirements, and may ask you to revise and resubmit.

- **Approved**: The work may be shown and downloaded on the Hub (subject to product rules);
- **Rejected**: We will explain the reason (e.g. policy violation, quality, unclear rights); you may fix issues and submit again.

Review timing depends on workload; we do not guarantee a specific timeline but will process the queue reasonably.

## 5. Prohibited conduct

- Repeated or abusive submissions, or manipulating likes or download counts;
- Uploading content substantially similar to others’ work without authorization;
- Using submissions for ads, unrelated external links, or non-wallpaper promotion;
- Attempting to bypass upload limits, review, or security measures.

Violations may result in takedown, account restrictions, or permanent bans.

## 6. Infringement complaints

If you believe a work on the Hub infringes your rights, email **codelume@163.com** with proof of rights, a link to the work, and contact information. We will handle valid notices as required by law.

## 7. Takedown and account action

We may remove content or restrict accounts without prior consent when:

- These guidelines, the Terms of Service, or law are violated;
- We receive a valid infringement notice;
- Technical or security risks exist;
- Accounts are unused for extended periods or system resources are abused.

## 8. Changes to these guidelines

We may update these guidelines and publish a new version. If you continue submitting after an update takes effect, you accept the update; if you disagree, stop submitting.
